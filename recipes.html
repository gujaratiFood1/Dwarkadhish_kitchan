Discover some of our favorite Gujarati and Indian dishes with simple steps to cook at home!

Dhokla

A fluffy and tangy Gujarati snack made from fermented rice and chickpea batter.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 tsp fruit salt (Eno)
  • Salt and sugar to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix besan, yogurt, ginger-chili paste, salt and sugar to a smooth batter.
  2. Add fruit salt just before steaming and pour into a greased tray.
  3. Steam for 15-20 minutes till cooked through.
  4. Cut into pieces and garnish with mustard seeds and coriander.

Khandvi

Soft and savory rolls made with gram flour and yogurt, seasoned with mustard seeds.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • Green chili paste
  • Salt and turmeric powder

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients with water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Cook on low heat stirring continuously till thick.
  3. Spread thinly on a flat surface, cut into strips and roll.
  4. Temper with mustard seeds and grated coconut.
